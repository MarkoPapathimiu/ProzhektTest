Apple Ricotta Pancakes

Nutrition Facts:

Calories: 344 kcal

Carbs: 37g

Protein: 13g

Fat: 17g

Recipe Directions:

In a medium mixing bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, ground cinnamon, baking soda.

In another bowl, whisk egg, buttermilk, ricotta, 1 tbsp sugar, and vanilla. Combine with dry ingredients. Let sit for 10 minutes.

Heat butter and oil in a skillet. Add 2 apple rings, cook 1 minute. Flip, add 1/4 cup batter, cook until bubbles form (2-4 minutes). Flip, sprinkle with cinnamon-sugar, cook until golden (2 minutes).

Chicken & Broccoli Salad

Nutrition Facts:

Calories: 394 kcal

Carbs: 7g

Protein: 37g

Fat: 25g

Recipe Directions:

In a large skillet over medium heat, cook chopped bacon until crisp (about 6 minutes).

Sprinkle chicken tenders with ¼ tsp salt and ¼ tsp pepper.

In the same skillet over medium heat, cook chicken 4–6 minutes per side until browned and cooked through (165°F internal temp).

Let rest 5 minutes, then cut or shred into bite-size pieces.

Carbs: 12g

Protein: 35g

Fat: 16g

Chicken Tortellini Soup

Nutrition Facts:

Calories: 435kcal

Carbs: 31g

Protein: 25g

Fat: 23g

Recipe Directions:

Cook onion, carrots, celery, and garlic in butter until soft; add tarragon, seasoning, pepper.

Add broth, bring to a boil, stir in tortellini, and cook covered until al dente (about 5 mins).

Add chicken, spinach, peas, and cream; cook until spinach wilts and peas are bright green (2–3 mins).
